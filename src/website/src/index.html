ACRONYM

A Complex Refrigerator and Organiser for Nutritious and Yummy Munchies

ACRONYM is an project for an Embedded Systems class at the University of Strathclyde Scotland. We have aimed to create a fridge system that will enable users, and carers to monitor the status and contents of the fridge
